FACILITIES

THE SANJEEVANI HOSPITAL

CASUALTY

Casualty beeps alert round the clock with expert health care team to meet any kind of service/care to all by respecting the value of life. We have in the casuality 7 beds, ready to give prompt service to the emergency cases.

PHARMACY

Pharmacy dispenses medicines at a reduced rate. The pharmacy store is given special attention to keep its temperature always cool.

PATHOLOGY DEPARTMENT

The modern equipments in the laboratory backs the functioning of the hospital for the speedy recovery of the patients. A full time pathologist and twelve technicians work here devoutedly.

BLOOD BANK

Hospital has a licensed Blood bank (Whole Blood and Components ) with modern machines and accurate reports. Since there are two blood banks (Component) in Ujjain it rendering its service with the support of Rotary Club. Blood bank is giving immense service to the patients and is an asset to the city.

NURSING SCHOOL

The nursing school run by the S. H Sisters of Ujjain Province can accommodate 20 new students every year. In the past few years two of the students were awarded with gold medals and three of them got first ranks. Nursing students are given exemplary training in the field of nursing in well furnished lab and classrooms.

CANTEEN

The newly inaugurated Canteen give exemplary food as per the diet proposed by the dietician. Emulent Water Treatment Plant- The main aim is to make use of the used water for the plants and the greenery around. The garden is watered with this water.

PARKING

We have spacious parking ground that can accommodate 150 two wheelers and 30 four wheelers. Healthy Atmosphere- Hospital has a very hygenic surrounding with trees and garden. Away from the pollution of the city, Pushpa Mission Hospital provides a very healthy atmosphere for the staff and patients.
