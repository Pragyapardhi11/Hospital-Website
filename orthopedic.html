HIGHLIGHTS

Orthopedists treat a wide variety of musculoskeletal conditions. These conditions may be present from birth, or they may result from an injury or age-related wear and tear. Below are some of the most common conditions that an orthopedist may treat:
  • joint pain from arthritis
  • bone fractures
  • soft tissue injuries, which are those that affect muscles, tendons, and ligaments back pain
  • overuse and sports injuries, including sprains, tendinitis, meniscus tears, and anterior cruciate ligament (ACL) tears
  • congenital conditions, such as clubfoot and scoliosis
