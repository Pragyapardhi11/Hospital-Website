ABOUT US


#PAST

SANJEEVANI CARE Hospital was founded on December 1, 1974. It was a time when no health care facilities were available in villages and no awareness of health care programmes were present. In the beginning stages the reverend sisters and doctors went to the villages and conducted many medical camps, supplied free medicines and tried to make them aware of the importance of health and dignity of human life. Thousands of patients in the villages were cured due to the selfless service of the doctors, unconditional commitment of the reverend sisters and nurses and the hospital began to be known far and wide. The hospital always led by a clear vision and mission and value system. His Excellency John Perumattam, Rev Fr John Plakeel took the initiative to start a hospital to serve the poor in the village.


#PRESENT

Pushpa Mission Hospital is a 146 bedded NABH (Entry Level) Certified multiy speciality hospital situated in the city of Ujjain. It has different department like Casualty, Medical & Surgical, Obstetrics & Gynecology, Orthopedics, Gastroentrology, Pulmonology, E.N.T., Eye, Pathology Lab, Endoscopy, 2d-Echocardiology, X-Ray & Ultrasound. There are 60 doctors rendering their service in different capacities in every medical field. The hospital is known for maternity care and care for the new borns. There are around 400 to 500 deliveries every month. It has 6 well furnished Operation Theaters and 2 minor Operation Theaters. Pushpa Mission Hospital also provide the facility of its own Blood Center (Sponsered by Rotary Club Ujjain) is the only second Blood Center with Components in Ujjain district.
